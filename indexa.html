Pricing


Monthly Memberships : These are the most common type of memberships, where you pay a monthly fee to access the gym's facilities and services. Monthly memberships often come with the flexibility to cancel or upgrade at any time.

Annual Memberships : Some gyms offer discounts if you commit to a year-long membership. This option can be cost-effective if you're confident you'll be using the gym consistently throughout the year.For those who don't want to commit to a regular membership, some gyms offer a pay-per-visit option. While this might be more expensive in the long run for frequent gym-goers, it can suit occasional users better.

Class-Based Memberships: Many gyms provide specialized classes like yoga, spinning, or dance. These classes may be included in the standard membership fee, or you might need to pay extra for them.

Premium Memberships: Some gyms offer premium memberships that provide access to additional amenities such as spa facilities, personal training sessions, or exclusive classes.
